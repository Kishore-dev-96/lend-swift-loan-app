Frequently Asked Questions

How long does loan approval take?

Most applications are processed within 24-48 hours. Instant pre-approval is available for eligible customers.

What documents do I need?

Typically, you'll need identity proof, address proof, income documents, and bank statements. Requirements may vary by loan type.

Can I prepay my loan?

Yes, you can prepay your loan anytime without penalty. This can help you save on interest.

Is my data secure?

We use military-grade encryption and comply with all data protection regulations. Your information is completely secure.

What if I'm rejected?

You'll receive detailed feedback on your application. Our loan advisors can suggest alternatives or areas to improve.

Do you offer EMI calculators?

Yes, our AI-powered EMI calculator provides instant calculations for different loan amounts and tenures.
